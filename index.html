Igor Sikorsky

Aviation pioneer

Igor Sikorsky's first helicopter ascent, Stratford, September 1939

Here's a time line of Igor Sikorsky life:

  • 1889 - An Aviation hero (Igor sikorsky) was born in Kiev, Russia
  • 1901 - When he was 12 he built own toy helicopter
  • 1903 - As a kid he was inspired by Leonardo Da Vinci's drawings
  • 1919 - He moved to the United States
  • 1923 - After immigrating to the United States in 1919, Sikorsky founded the Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation.
  • 1939 - Sikorsky designed and flew the Vought-Sikorsky VS-300, the first viable American helicopter, which pioneered the rotor configuration used by most helicopters today.
  • 1944 - He has made a 4 engine airplane.
  • 1956 - Sikorsky Aircraft moved its headquarters to an 800,000 square foot plant on North Main Street in Stratford, Connecticut.
  • 1972 - Igor Sikorsky died of old age at the age of 83. After a normal day at united aircraft.
